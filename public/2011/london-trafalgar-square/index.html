London - Trafalgar Square at Night

Mar 21st, 2011

trafalgar square london

I woke up Saturday morning in a London hostel and then proceeded to spend the next 10 hours touring the city - mostly on foot. The entire time I lugged around my tripod wondering if I should have left the thing at home.

Then the sun set and I was treated to London at night in the form of Trafalagar Square making my sore shoulders and feet worth it.
