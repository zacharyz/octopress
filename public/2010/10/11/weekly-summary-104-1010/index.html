Weekly Summary 10/4 - 10/10

Oct 11th, 2010

Summary

2,270 calories under budget for the week. Lost 2 pounds this week.
This week was surprising in that I managed to lose weight despite exercising less. During a run early in the week my left calf cramped up and popped, which as you can imagine was quite alarming. That was enough to tell me to listen to my body and to take it easy for the rest of the week. This week I don’t plan on going running every day like I have been to give my body some time to repair itself. I will be attending more jiu jitsu though and doing more kettle bell training.

Almost half-way there!
